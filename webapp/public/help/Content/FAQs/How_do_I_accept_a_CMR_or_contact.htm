How do I accept a CMR or contact?

To accept assignment of a CMR or a contact event:

  1. Click Events in the upper right corner of the page.
  2. Review the list and locate the morbidity event or contact you want to accept.
  3. Click the Accept radio button in the row of the event you want to accept.
