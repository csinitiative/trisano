How do I delete a contact?

You can delete contacts in different places, depending on their routing status:

  • If the contact has been routed, it will appear on the Events list and you can delete it right from the list:
    1. Click Events in the upper right corner of the page.
    2. Locate the contact that you want to delete.
    3. Click Delete. A message appears asking you to confirm that you want to delete the contact.
    4. To confirm that you want to delete the contact, click Yes. To cancel the deletion, click No. Deleted contacts are still listed on the Events list, but they are listed in gray, italic type.
  • If the contact has not been routed, you can delete it from the Contacts tab of the CMR it belongs to.
    1. Click Events in the upper right corner of the page.
    2. Locate the Morbidity event that the contact belongs to and click Show.
    3. Click Delete. A message appears asking you to confirm that you want to delete the contact.
    4. To confirm that you want to delete the contact, click Yes. To cancel the deletion, click No. Deleted contacts are still listed on the Contacts tab of the CMR they belong to, but they are listed with strike-through characters.

If you confirm deletion of a contact:

  • it remains in the database
  • it appears as dimmed in the event list or as strike-through characters elsewhere
  • it can still be viewed using the Show command
  • it is included in searches
  • it appears with strike-through out in search results
  • it is not exported to the CDC or to an IBIS query

In addition, you can still use deleted contacts for the following:

  • you can edit deleted contacts
  • you can route deleted contacts
