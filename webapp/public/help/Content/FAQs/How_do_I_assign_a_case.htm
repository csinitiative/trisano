How do I assign a case?

In CSI TriSano™, cases are referred to as CMRs (confidential morbidity reports) or morbidity events. To assign an event:

  1. Click Events in the upper right corner of the page.
  2. Review the list and locate the event you want to assign.
  3. Click Route to Local Health Depts. in the row of the event you want to assign.
  4. Select a jurisdiction to assign the case to from the Investigating jurisdiction drop-down list.
  5. If you want other jurisdictions to have access to the event, select additional jurisdictions from the Also grant access to list.
  6. Enter a note, if necessary, in the Brief Note field.
  7. Click Route Event.
