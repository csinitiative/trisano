About Jurisdictions

Jurisdictions describe areas of authority that are usually based on geography, such as county. In terms of public health and CSI TriSano™, a Jurisdiction is an entity who has authority by assignment in various areas of the application. Once a Jurisdiction has been defined:

  • you can add counties to it in the system codes area
  • you can route CMRs and contacts to a Jurisdiction
  • administrators can assign user roles by Jurisdiction
  • you can define forms for the Jurisdiction
