Deleting a Disease

CSI TriSano does not allow you to delete a disease once you have saved it. There are a few different reasons for this:

  • Deleting the disease would require deleting the events associated with it. The event information contains useful data.
  • The CDC may want information collected on that disease. Deleting the disease would prevent you from reporting useful information about it.
