Assigning a Form to an Event

Form assignment refers to the relationship between an event, (CMR, Contact, Encounter, or Place) and a published form. Assignment happens when the form is connected to a particular event. This normally occurs automatically, but you can override automatic assignment and manage it manually, if you prefer.

Automatically Assigning a Form

Forms are automatically assigned to an event in the following ways:

At event-creation time – when the event is saved for the first time

  • If the event has a disease associated with it, any forms associated with that disease and the event's jurisdictions are assigned to the form. No other form will be automatically assigned to this event in the future.
  • If the event does not have a disease associated with it, no form is assigned.

At event-update time – when the event is saved in the future

  • If the event has a disease associated with it and a form has not been previously assigned, any forms associated with that disease and the event's jurisdictions are assigned to the form. No other form will be automatically assigned to this event in the future.
  • If a disease is still not associated with the event, then no form assignment occurs and the event remains eligible for automatic form assignment.

Note: If you change the disease assigned to an even after you have already saved the event, the application does not change the forms assigned to it. You must change the form assignment manually.

Manually Assigning a Form to an Event

To manually assign a form:

  1. Click Add/Remove forms for this event.







  2. The page that opens contains form names that are available for this event. The page has two sections:
  • Forms in use – can be removed from the event
  • Forms available for use – can be added to the event

  1. To add a form, if there are any listed, select the check box on the right side of the page and click Add Forms. The form is added to the section called Forms in Use.
