Exporting a Form

When you exporting a form, you save it in a compressed format to the location of your choice. Once you have exported the form, you can then upload it to another installation of CSI TriSano™.

 

To export a form:

  1. Click Admin in the upper right corner of the page.
  2. Click Manage Forms under Disease Specific Forms. The Forms list opens.
  3. Locate the form you want to export.
  4. Click Export in the same row as the form. A dialog opens and based on your operating system, asks you questions about whether to Open or Save the file.
  5. Select Save, navigate to the place where you want to save the file and click OK.
