Uploading a Form

You can upload forms that were created by another installation of CSI TriSano™.

 

To upload a form:

  1. Click Admin in the upper right corner of the page.
  2. Click Manage Forms under Disease Specific Forms. The Forms list opens.
  3. Click Browse under the Create new form button, to locate the form you want to upload and select it; or if you know the location of the form, enter it into the text field to the left of the Browse button.

  4. Click Open. The name of the form appears in the text field to the left of the Browse button.

  5. To finish uploading the file, click Upload. After you have uploaded the form, the Detail View of the form is displayed. You can now edit and publish the form.
