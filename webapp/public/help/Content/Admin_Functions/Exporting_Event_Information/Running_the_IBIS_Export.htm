Running the IBIS Export

IBIS stands for Indicator-Based Information System. It is a standard of measure used in evaluating public health. The information in an IBIS report is used to: 

  • Track and evaluate progress toward goals.
  • Guide policy decisions, priorities and long-range strategic plans.
  • Develop, focus, and streamline data collection and reporting capacity.
  • Provide comprehensive information on your jurisdiction's health and health care system.

To run the report:

  1. Click Admin in the upper right corner of the page.
  2. Under Export Event Information, click IBIS Export. The export page opens.
  3. Enter a Start Date and End Date for the report. You must enter dates in the format YYYY-MM-DD. No other format is accepted in these fields.
  4. Click Submit.
  5. Click Save and specify where you want to save the exported information.
  6. Click Save once again to finish saving the export.
