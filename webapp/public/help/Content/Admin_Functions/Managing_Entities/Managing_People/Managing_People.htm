Managing People

The People list displays all patients, contacts, and clinicians whose names are stored in the database. The Manage People features allow you as an administrator to keep the database of names organized and clean of duplicates and mistakes. To view the People list:

  1. Click People in the upper right corner of the page.
  2. The People page opens displaying one person per row, sorted alphabetically by last name. Each listing shows:
  • person's name
  • address details
  • birth date
