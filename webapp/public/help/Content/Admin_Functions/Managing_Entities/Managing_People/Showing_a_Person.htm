Showing a Person

To show a person:

  1. Click People in the upper right corner of the page.
  2. Review the list and locate the person you want to view.
  3. Click Show.

The page displays the demographic information for this person in show mode, meaning that the data is not available for editing. Switch to edit mode by clicking Edit if you want to edit the information for this person.

 

In show mode, the only function that is available to you is Lookup Address.
