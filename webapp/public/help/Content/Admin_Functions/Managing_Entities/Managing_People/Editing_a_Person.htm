Editing a Person

The edit people function allows you to clean up the people records in your database, without having to go through the events page. To edit a person:

  1. Click People in the upper right corner of the page. The People list opens.
  2. Review the list or use the search tool to locate the person you want to edit.
  3. Click Edit. The page displays the demographic information for this person in edit mode.
  4. Make any necessary changes and click Save & Exit. The application displays a message telling you that the person was successfully updated.
  5. Click Back to People to return to the people list.
