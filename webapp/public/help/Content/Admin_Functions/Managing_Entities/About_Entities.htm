About Entities

Entities are the people and places that exist within CSI TriSano™.

  • People are the patients, contacts, and clinicians whose names are stored in the database. The term people does not refer to users of the application.
  • Places are the locations such as hospitals and clinics where people are treated as well as places where possible exposure took place.

CSI TriSano™ allows you as an administrator to work with people and place names to help ensure a high level of data integrity. Entity Management lets you:

  • Edit a person's name
  • Show a person's name
  • Create a new person
  • Create a CMR based on a person
  • Merge one or more persons with a selected person
  • Edit an address
  • Merge one or more existing addresses with a selected address
