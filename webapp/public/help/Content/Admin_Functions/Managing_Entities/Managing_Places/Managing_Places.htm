Managing Places

A place can be any of the following:

  • diagnostic facility
  • hospitalization facility
  • lab
  • place exposure
  • reporting agency

 

The Place Management page lets you search for places and then edit or merge place names. To reach the Manage Places page:

  1. Click Places in the upper right corner of the page. The Manage Places page opens displaying its search options.
