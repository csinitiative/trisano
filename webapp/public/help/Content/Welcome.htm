Welcome to CSI TriSano™

CSI TriSano™ is a surveillance and outbreak management system for infectious disease, environmental hazards, and bio-terrorism attacks. It allows local, state, and federal entities to track, control and ultimately prevent illness and death.

CSI TriSano™ provides timely and efficient collection, interpretation, and dissemination of data, a critical component of surveillance. CSI TriSano™ role-based routing and queuing features enable surveillance data to be shared with those responsible for disease prevention and control to facilitate public health activities.

CSI TriSano™ provides the tools to input and manage investigation-related tasks, follow-up encounters with patients, and clinical notes.

The CSI TriSano™ dashboard allows investigators to log in to the system, view the day's tasks and document notes and activities associated with those tasks and encounters.

With CSI TriSano™, you can:

  • Enter and track confidential morbidity reports
  • Manage contact, encounters, and place exposures
  • Create custom forms for tracking events
  • Export reports to CSV format files
  • Analyze, visualize, and report on events
  • Continually maintain and monitor events with high availability

Use this help system to learn about the different functions of CSI TriSano™. Additional information is available on our subscription area.
