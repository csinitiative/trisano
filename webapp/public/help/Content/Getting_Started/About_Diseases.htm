About Diseases

CSI TriSano™ empowers local and state health departments to provide better public health services through surveillance and outbreak management. CSI TriSano™ lets you define and track emergent diseases and conditions of interest on the local level. This means, for example, that you could define lead poisoning as a disease and use CSI TriSano™ to follow up and investigate cases related to it. You can also collect information on diseases formally identified by the CDC. Any of the information collected can be shared with the CDC on a case-by-case basis.
