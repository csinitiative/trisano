What is an Encounter Event?

An encounter is a meeting between clinical staff and a patient. An Encounter event records the following details about that meeting:

  • the investigator who is interviewing the patient
  • the date the encounter took place
  • the location where the encounter took place
  • a description of the encounter

Encounter events are displayed on the Encounter tab of a morbidity event. An Encounter event includes the following tabs:

  • Encounter information
  • Clinical information
  • Laboratory information
  • Investigation information
  • Notes
