What is a Contact Event?

A contact is a person who the patient may have been in close proximity with, may have caught something from or may have exposed to an illness. Collecting contact information allows health officials to track as many forms of exposure and contact as possible.

A contact event is a collection of information about a person the patient came in contact with. It includes tabs that collect information about the contact in the same way as the tabs for morbidity events. The tabs for a contact event are:

  • Demographic information
  • Clinical information
  • Laboratory information 
  • Epidemiological information 
  • Investigation information
  • Notes
