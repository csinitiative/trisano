Promoting a Contact Event to a Morbidity Event

Contact events list data for a person who has had contact with the patient. Sometimes, it becomes necessary to change the contact event to a CMR. You can do this using the Promote to CMR command. To promote a contact event to a CMR:

  1. Click Events in the upper right corner of the page.
  2. Locate the row for the contact event that you want to promote.
  3. Click Show.
  4. Click Promote to CMR. A confirmation box appears asking if you are sure you want to promote the event.
  5. Click Yes to make the promotion, or click No to cancel it.
  6. If you click Yes, a CMR page opens with the saved information for what was previously a contact event. The event is no longer a contact event, but is now a morbidity event. The contact event information remains on the CMR for the original patient and the original patient relationship is noted on the Contact tab of the new morbidity event.
