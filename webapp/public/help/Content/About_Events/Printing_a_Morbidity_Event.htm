Printing a Morbidity or Contact Event

To print a morbidity or contact event:

  1. Click Events in the upper right corner of the page.
  2. Review the list and locate the event you want to print.
  3. Click Print. A small window opens that lets you choose to print the information from any of the event's tabs.
  4. Select the information you want to print and click Print. The information for the event prints.

Note: Attachments are not printed when you print an event.
