What is a Place Exposure?

A Place Exposure describes a location where a patient may have been in close proximity with an illness or may have been exposed to an illness. Collecting place information allows health officials to track as many forms of exposure as possible. Place exposures are listed on the Epidemiological tab of a morbidity event. They include:

  • the name of the place
  • the type of place
  • the date of exposure

Place Exposures include the following tabs:

  • Place information
  • Investigation information
  • Notes
