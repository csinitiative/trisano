Deleting a Place Exposure

To delete a place exposure that is part of a morbidity event:

  1. Click Events in the upper right corner of the page.
  2. Review the list and locate the morbidity event that contains the place exposure you want to delete.
  3. Click Edit. The page for the morbidity event opens.
  4. Select the Epidemiological tab.
  5. Locate the place exposure you want to delete. Select the Remove check box.
  6. Click Save & Continue. The place exposure is deleted and no longer visible.
