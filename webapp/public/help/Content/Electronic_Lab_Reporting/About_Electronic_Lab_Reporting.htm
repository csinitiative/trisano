Electronic Lab Reporting

The Electronic Lab Reporting (ELR) tools allow CSI TriSano™ to receive electronically transmitted lab results and then connect them to events for either new or existing patients. Many labs send their reports in electronic formats. These reports can be used by the application to save you a great deal of time and effort in tracking lab data.

With the ELR Management tool, you can:

  • View lab results in a staging area by their status: Pending, Assigned, Discarded, Unprocessable
  • Match lab results to existing morbidity events or contact events
  • Update a morbidity event or contact event with lab results
  • Create a new event with lab results
  • Discard lab results

With the ELR Administration tool, you can:

  • Manage common test types
  • Manage LOINC codes
  • Manage organisms
  • Link one or more LOINC codes to common test types
  • Link one or more LOINC codes to an organism
  • Associate common test types with diseases
  • Associate one or more organisms with a disease
