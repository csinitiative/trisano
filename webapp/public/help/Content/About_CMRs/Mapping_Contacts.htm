Mapping Contacts

The mapping feature allows you to display a map that contains tags showing the location of the patient and any of the contacts associated with the patient. This allows you to see in a graphical way, the proximity of the people who have been in contact with the patient.

 

To map contacts:

  1. Click Events in the upper right corner of the page.
  2. Locate the patient whose contacts you want to map and click either Show or Edit. You do not have to be in Edit Mode to map the contacts for the patient.
  3. Select the Contacts tab. You'll see the contacts for the patient listed as in the following illustration.

 

 

  1. Click Show Contacts Map. A map of the patient and the listed contacts opens just below the contact list as shown in the following illustration. The red tag indicates the address of the patient and the blue tags indicate the addresses of the contacts.

 

 

Note: If any of the patient's contacts are missing map coordinates, you'll see a message next to the Show Contacts Map link indicating this as shown in the following illustration. When you see this message, you will have to check each of the contacts and update their coordinates before that contact will appear on the map.
