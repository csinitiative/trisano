Epidemiological Information

Epidemiological information records and tracks some of the factors relating to how the patient was exposed and what the possible causes are for the current condition. Use the Epidemiological tab to record information about contacts and place exposures for the patient. Enter as much information as possible.

Note: Depending on the disease you select, different questions appear on the CMR tabs. These questions are specific to the disease and appear once you have made the choice and selected one of the Save options. For more information about diseases, read Choosing a Disease and Editing a Disease.

Mapping Addresses

After you have entered and saved address information for a place exposure, you may see a link at the bottom of the Address area called Look Up Address. This feature allows you to check the address of this place for validity. In addition, once you have checked the address, you can show it on a map. Refer to Mapping Place Exposures for more information.
