Confidential Morbidity Report

A confidential morbidity report (CMR) is a collection of information that describes an encounter with a patient that must be reported to a public health department. The report includes details that public health authorities use in order to track disease occurrences and analyze patterns. The details include:

  • Demographic information
  • Clinical information
  • Laboratory information
  • Contacts information
  • Encounter notes
  • Epidemiological information
  • Reporting details
  • Investigation information
  • Notes
  • Administrative information
