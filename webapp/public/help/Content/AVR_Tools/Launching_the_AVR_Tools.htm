Launching the AVR Tools

The AVR tool is installed and configured for your organization by your IT staff. These instructions assume that Pentaho™ has been properly installed and configured.

 

To launch the AVR Tools:

  1. Click AVR in the upper right corner of CSI TriSano™. This opens a page that welcomes you to Pentaho™.
  2. Click the Pentaho™ User Login button. A small login window opens.
  3. Type your username and password. If you do not know your username and password, check with your IT staff.
  4. If you prefer, select Launch in new window.
  5. Click OK.

Pentaho™ opens in your window of choice, either within the CSI TriSano™ application or in a separate window. Launching Pentaho™ in a new window give you more screen space.
