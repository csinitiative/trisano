CollabNet project pages make it easier for you to write, track and share code. You can use CollabNet project pages to manage or contribute to a software development project at your own pace and on your own terms. This brief guide provides some initial steps to get you started.

Step 1: Join a project.

To work on a project in CollabNet project pages, you must join the project.

Step 2: Communicate with others on the project.

You can stay in touch with other people on the project by joining mailing lists, participating in discussions, and following project announcements.

Step 3: Look at the code.

You can use your browser to review code that's been committed to your project's repository.

Step 4: Contribute to the project.

When you have joined a project, contacted your co-workers, and connected to the repository, you are ready to contribute to your project.
