You can invite people to join your project or just add them to your membership, depending on your organization's practices.

To invite a user to join your project, follow these steps:

  1. Under Project tools, click Membership ⇒ Invite new members.
  2. In the People to invite field, list the email addresses of one or more potential participants.
  3. In the Invitation message field, provide a reason for joining the project.
  4. Click Invite.

To add a user to your project, follow these steps:

  1. Under Project tools, click Membership ⇒ Add new member/role.
  2. Add the the user by selecting Add.
  3. Select the role you wish to grant.
  4. Click Grant roles.

Note: To add multiple users at once, list their user names in the Mass add field. Select one or more roles for each of the new members in the Grant these roles field, then click Grant roles. Users must already be registered on the domain to be eligible.
